Reflect Simple

Write in one place, keep your thoughts organized, share on your favorite platforms and reflect on life

The Story

I was constantly writing on different things - such as wordpress, twitter, my journal, notes on iphone and even random paper. If I needed to find a note I wrote down, chances are I wouldn't be able to find it.

As I was noticing this trend I was currently working on another app. One that would help you reflect on life every week, month and year. It would be a reminder to sit and be remembered of your life. This is a life tool that I picked up a few years ago, but has always been difficult to stay consistent.

I worked on this life review reminder application for quite some time, talked to a few people about it, and eventually stopped working on it. Just like many of my other projects.

About half a year later, I still desired to have something similar and started to rethink my approach. This is when Reflect Simple was born.

The idea is this, a web and ios app that is two things. One, a non distracting way to take notes, thoughts and blog posts and categorize them so I can easily find them later as well as post to some of my favorite social media outlets. And two, a way to reflect on that I have written. I decided to create a book out of my notes. Then when I receive the book in the mail, I simply sit down and read it, reflect and grow.

This tool I hope to be helpful to many. As I continue to build it and work out the details, sign up and stay in the loop.
